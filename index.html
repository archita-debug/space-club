CGU Space Club

Welcome to the CGU Space Club – where curiosity meets cosmos. Join us to explore space, astronomy, and aerospace technology through hands-on projects and events.

About Us

We are a passionate community of students dedicated to exploring space, astronomy, and cutting-edge aerospace technology.

From stargazing nights and rocket-building workshops to space research and competitions, we aim to ignite innovation and curiosity beyond Earth.

Our club provides hands-on experience through collaborative projects, tech talks, and space simulations.

Whether you're a space enthusiast or a future aerospace engineer, there's a place for you here.

Join us as we explore the universe—one project, one mission, one launch at a time.
